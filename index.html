About Mischka

“People don’t use a product because of its great design; Great design helps them use the product”
- Viran Dayaratne

Hi, Welcome!

I’m Mischka Kruger, a self-taught UX Designer based in Pretoria, South Africa. I specialize in creating beautiful user experience designs in the Automotive industry and taught myself how to code by reverse engineering websites I admire, attending webinars, doing online courses and reading interesting articles online. I use a variety of proven techniques (one is User-empathy) and theories that can help your company towards success and reaching your goals. I love the work that I get to do... I consider it my calling and I’m excited to get to know you!
